# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Health-conscious parents and caregivers in **Saudi Arabia (KSA)** shopping on mobile for a children's multivitamin. Site is **Arabic-first** with English toggle; **KSA-only** checkout (SAR). They are often dealing with picky eaters, taste refusal, or gummy fatigue (sugar, missing iron, daily battles). Context: evening kitchen routine, quick decisions on phone, high trust bar for anything that goes in a child's food.

## Product Purpose

**blendavit** is a DTC marketing site for a tasteless powder multivitamin (30 × 1g sachets, Toddlers 1–3 and Kids 4+). The site must:

1. Explain why powder + liposomal iron beats gummies (evidence-led, SFDA-registered).
2. Drive **reserve / waitlist checkout** (Shopify) to validate demand before manufacturing.
3. Support **KSA only (SAR)** at launch on this site (USA deferred to a separate pass if needed).
4. Anchor the parent brand **Smart Mom Labs** without launching the full 12-SKU hub yet.

Success: paid reservations and clear variant/market selection, not vanity traffic.

## Brand Personality

**Confident, clinical, calm.** Premium family wellness (not loud "kids brand" candy aesthetics). EllaOla-style energy: bold product grid, trust credentials up front, parent-first copy. Warmth comes from photography and reassurance, not pastel clichés.

## Anti-references

- Generic supplement landing (mint wash + Cormorant/DM Sans + three identical quote cards).
- Luna & Eve **layout clone** without blendavit differentiation (iron-in-sachet story must lead).
- Fake named doctor endorsements without signed advisors.
- Shopper-visible dev/Shopify setup notes.
- Disease-treatment claims; buzzword cadence ("transform", "revolution", "seamless").
- Emoji icon grids on a clinical SFDA brand.

## Design Principles

1. **Evidence before adjectives** — Kutbi, iron, 0g sugar claims are cited or framed as formulation rationale.
2. **One primary action** — Reserve your box; variant and market visible before checkout.
3. **Format is the product** — Tasteless sachet + iron is the hero story, not abstract "wellness."
4. **Market is explicit** — Kingdom of Saudi Arabia only; no USA/USD selector on site.
5. **Honest waitlist** — No fake in-stock shipping; deposits explained when enabled.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Target **WCAG 2.1 AA** (contrast on mint-tinted backgrounds, focus states on tabs/buttons).
- **Arabic-first (RTL)** with English toggle; both ship on static site via `i18n.js`.
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` for any animation added later.
- Supplement disclaimer visible in footer on all pages.
