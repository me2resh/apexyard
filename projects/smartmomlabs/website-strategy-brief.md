## 1. Competitive / reference brief — Luna & Eve vs blendavit

### Borrow from Luna & Eve

| Pattern | L&E execution | blendavit use |
|--------|----------------|---------------|
| Tasteless format as hero | “Your child won't notice it's there” | Lead with *zero cooperation required* |
| Expert trust scaffold | Nutritionist / dietitian quotes above fold | Paediatrician Approved + 1–2 GCC clinical quotes |
| FAQ as objection handler | Long anticipatory FAQ | GCC: halal, SFDA, mixing habits, school bag |
| Subscription CTA | “20% off Subscribe & Save” in header | Monthly sachet = natural subscription unit |
| Risk reversal | 30-day money-back | Same — reduces first-purchase friction |
| Sachet illustration | Product + open sachet | Show individual foil sachet (size matters) |
| Benefit clusters | Brain / Immunity / Mood icons | Growth / Bones / Vision / Immunity (on-pack) |
| Vegan + Halal badges | Footer + PDP | Halal above-fold for GCC |

### Diverge from Luna & Eve

| Dimension | L&E | blendavit |
|-----------|-----|-----------|
| Age range | 1–3 toddlers | 4+ Kids (+ Toddlers 1–3 SKU) |
| Evidence | “Developed with paediatricians” (advisory) | Grade A/B RCT citations — clinical brand |
| Iron | No iron in powder | Liposomal iron (Lipofer) — **homepage claim**, not buried |
| Sugar | Implied clean | Head-to-head: 8g gummy vs 0g blendavit (AHA stat) |
| Market | UK / NHS | GCC: Kutbi 2019, SFDA, local pain points |
| Tone | Soft UK pastel | Confident: smart parent as decision-maker |
| Parent brand | Single brand | Smart Mom Labs portfolio depth |

**Key moat vs L&E:** L&E cannot carry iron in tasteless powder without metallic taste; blendavit’s Lipofer microencapsulation is the technical differentiator — *“iron that gummies can never give them.”*

### Pages

| Page | Purpose |

### Home sections (outline)

1. **Hero** — *The only complete multivitamin your child will never refuse.* Sub: tasteless / colourless / odourless; 30×1g. CTAs: Shop Now; Subscribe & Save 20%. Trust ribbon: Paediatrician Approved · SFDA · Halal · 0g sugar.
2. **Proof hook** — 89.8% GCC taste resistance (Kutbi 2019); gummy 8g sugar vs 0g.
3. **Format table** — Gummy vs blendavit (iron, sugar, cooperation, detectability).
4. **How it works** — Open → pour into any food/drink → done.
5. **Benefits** — Growth / Bones / Vision / Immunity icons.
6. **Ingredients** — 12 nutrients; Lipofer iron highlight; age toggle 1–3 / 4+.
7. **Social proof** — GCC experts + parent UGC.
8. **Trust** — Money-back, batch testing, SFDA, halal, made for GCC.
9. **FAQ** — Include WhatsApp support expectation.
10. **Footer** — Newsletter 10% off; subscribe mechanics.

### CTAs (priority order depends on channel decision)

1. Buy (single)
2. Subscribe & Save 20%
3. Find a retailer (if pharmacy-led)
4. Contact / WhatsApp

- Clear ages 4+ / 1–3 on PDP
- Sachet usage education
- SFDA number when available
- Halal badge
