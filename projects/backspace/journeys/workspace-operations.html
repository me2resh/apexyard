Workspace Operations Journey

Staff-only Visit-first journey from arrival through checkout, payment/receivable, and space readiness. Add-ons are contextual Charges, not standalone anonymous POS sales.
DRAFT - preview before implementation

Login

staff

Staff authenticate through Better Auth before entering the console.

Page contents

  • Email and password form.
  • Error state for invalid credentials.
  • Redirect to Today after session is established.

Today Dashboard

staff

Operational launchpad for arrivals, active visits, bookings, open bills, spaces, cleaning, maintenance, and approvals.

Page contents

  • KPI cards for occupancy, active visits, revenue, open bills, upcoming bookings, overdue sessions.
  • Quick actions for staff workflows.
  • Queues for cleaning, maintenance, approvals, expiring memberships.

New Visit / Check-in

staff

Staff search or quick-create a person and choose the visit type and billing responsibility.

Page contents

  • Person search by phone/name.
  • Visit type and billing responsibility selectors.
  • Host, membership, booking, or event context when relevant.

Space Selection

staff

Staff pick an available space when the visit needs a Usage Session.

Page contents

  • Space Map by branch/floor/status.
  • Availability and double-booking warnings.
  • Maintenance, cleaning, blocked, inactive, occupied, and reserved states.

Active Visit

staff

Staff monitor the visit, session duration, charges, responsibility, and allowed actions.

Page contents

  • Visit details drawer.
  • Usage Session timer and overtime signals.
  • Charge list, responsibility badges, and audit timeline.

Add Charge

staff

Staff add products, services, fees, discounts, or complimentary lines to an operational target without standalone POS.

Page contents

  • Required target: visit, usage session, event, host account, or invoice draft.
  • Catalog search and manual charge option.
  • Stock check, responsibility selector, discount/override reason.

Checkout

staff

Staff preview usage, coverage, charges, discounts, tax, responsibility split, invoice(s), and payment requirements.

Page contents

  • Usage/session totals.
  • Membership or included coverage.
  • Responsibility split and open shift validation.

Payment / Receivable

staff

Staff record internal payment method/reference or confirm pay-later/host/event responsibility.

Page contents

  • Cash, card terminal, wallet, bank transfer, Instapay, mixed, pay-later, host account.
  • Shift requirement for cash.
  • Generated invoice/receipt summary.

Facilities Readiness

facilities

Checkout closes the session and moves the space to cleaning, maintenance, or available according to rules.

Page contents

  • Space state change.
  • Cleaning task creation.
  • Maintenance ticket path if damage or block reason exists.

Reports / Admin

manager

Managers review daily operations, occupancy, revenue, open receivables, staff roles, settings, approvals, and audit log.

Page contents

  • Daily, occupancy, revenue, receivables reports.
  • Staff roles, branch access, permission settings.
  • Audit log and approval queue.
